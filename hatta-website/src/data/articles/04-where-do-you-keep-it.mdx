## This summer I was working on the Yale Building Project and came across some scraps of cedar used for the house cladding.

They recycle old building materials in downtown Brooklyn and create furniture pieces with tons of character.
It’s kind of poetic from an architect’s point of view to allow architectural elements to live again in another designed fashion.
